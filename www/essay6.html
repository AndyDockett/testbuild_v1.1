ESSAY
MORTEN SVENSTRUP

...It’s bad if you lose your data, but if the iPad breaks and your insurance pays for a new one, you’re almost happy. Each iPad is indistinguishable from the next — thus an iPad is replaceable. In other words, we don’t think of the iPad as a physical object, but as a form, an idea, almost as a number. The most important aspect of the iPad is the screen, which of course is transparent. This clearly shows that it’s not the iPad as an object that interests us, but rather the signs and languages we can read on it...
