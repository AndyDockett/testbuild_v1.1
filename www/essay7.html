ESSAY
MORTEN SVENSTRUP

...If this is a trend — that in 2012 we begin to understand our environment and the objects in it as abstract, as interchangeable and as more or less non-material — what does that say about the way we live our lives? The idea is not so strange, certainly not considering how many hours a day we spend in front of our computer and television screens — in worlds that are no longer physical, yet designed by humans.

These worlds are built on the foundation of a material that is non-material in its essence — namely numbers.

Morten Svenstrup has a master’s degree from The Department of
Arts and Cultural Studies at the University of Copenhagen.
